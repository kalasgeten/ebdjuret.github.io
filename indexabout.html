I'm Ebba Alling, an Illustrator based in Stockholm, Sweden. This website is for all my personal work, projects and sketches. Storytelling is a central inspiration for my work, often with a playful angle. My style is diverse, with a mix of handmade and digital techniques.

I'm currently studying Graphical Design and Illustration at Konstfack University Of Arts, Crafts and Design. I'm always interested in collborations and inquiries. I’d love to hear from you!
